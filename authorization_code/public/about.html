Genre Explorer was formulated in the sleep deprived stages of a classic Silicon Valley hackathon, then painstakingly worked on by its two clinically insane founders until the following site resulted. This is a new take on music exploration, with our service finding similarly styled music across new genres of the user's picking based on a seed song, all running through the spoofy APIs on a Scratch (from Scratch in Scratch) backend with this brilliantly styled text brought to you by Handlebars, CSS 2.1, HTML4 (AKA Engrish), and a non-liberal Sysadmin.
